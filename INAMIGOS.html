Who We Are

A Grassroots Force for Good

InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered non-profit organisation dedicated to improving lives through education, social welfare, environmental conservation, animal welfare, and community development.

Since our founding in 2020, we have grown from a small volunteer circle into a structured organisation running several impactful programmes across India, always driven by the belief that collective action creates lasting change.

What We Do

Ongoing Projects

Environment

Project Prakriti

Large-scale tree plantation drives and environmental sustainability campaigns for a greener tomorrow.

Social Welfare

Project Seva

Regular food drives and essential support systems for underprivileged communities across the region.

Empowerment

Project Udaan

Awareness campaigns and skill-building workshops to empower girls and women towards independence.

Animal Welfare

Project Jeev

Daily feeding, medical aid, and care initiatives for stray and injured animals in urban neighbourhoods.
